the work remains

Every mark leaves a trace. This is the archive — five projects, one visual language — identities, print and digital worlds built to outlive the moment they were seen in.

Visual identity and packaging for a niche wine label from Goriška Brda. Logotype, leaf illustrations and numbered labels — a mark pressed into paper the way a vintage is pressed into memory.

A booklet marking 100 years since the passing of Srečko Kosovel — his constructivist kons poems set back into motion through typography, colour and rhythm.

editorialtypographyprint

Identity and packaging for a niche Mediterranean botanical aperitif. An arch, a sun-warmed palette and a label built for slow evenings — smooth ritual, not the rush.

identitylabelpackagingart direction

Identity, story and packaging for a family olive grove in the Vipava valley. Two olives form a gaze — generations of careful selection pressed into a single mark, treated like a premium wine label rather than a local etiquette.

Identity and website for personal trainer Žan Hočevar Peganc. A training system translated into a digital one — sharp type, acid accents, motion with intent. Built to move.

the designer

domen markeljc

I design identities, websites and digital brand experiences for brands with ambition. Clear enough to understand. Refined to feel premium. Distinct to be remembered.

My work blends strategy, typography, image, motion and interaction — not as separate pieces, but as one visual system with a clear emotional direction.

I do not create brands that simply look good for a moment. I create visual worlds that feel intentional, recognizable and remembered.
